# Founding Business Research Outreach

This wording is prepared for review. Sending it externally remains an approval-gated action because it represents the product owner and contacts real businesses.

## Email or longer direct message

**Subject:** Local business research for OurValleys

Hi [Name],

I’m developing OurValleys, a new independent local platform for Rhondda Cynon Taf. The idea is to help residents find useful local businesses and information while giving participating businesses a simple website and local profile that they can update in one place.

I’m speaking with a small number of local business owners before the website is built properly. I’m not selling anything at this stage—I want to understand how businesses currently manage their website, Google and social media information, what takes too much time, and whether the proposed service would solve a real problem.

Would you be open to a 20–30 minute conversation? I can also prepare a private example page using information you approve, so you can judge the idea properly rather than commenting on a vague concept.

There is no obligation to join or pay for anything. Any page would remain private unless you explicitly approved publication.

Thanks,

Rhys
OurValleys

## Outreach rules

- Personalise the reason for contacting the business.
- Do not imply council affiliation or endorsement.
- Do not claim the platform already has an audience.
- Do not promise customers, rankings, revenue or a launch date.
- Do not use scraped personal mobile numbers or personal email addresses without a reviewed lawful basis.
- Keep an accurate suppression list and honour requests not to be contacted.
- Limit follow-ups and avoid bulk unsolicited messaging.
- Store the candidate list outside the public repository.
